About the Community in Victoria, Laguna

Victoria is a 2nd class municipality in the province of Laguna, known for its rich agricultural lands, vibrant culture, and warm-hearted people. It proudly holds the title "Duck Raising Capital of the Philippines" due to its widespread and successful itik farming industry.
