Supporting Sustainable Agriculture for the Farmers of Victoria, Laguna

The Municipality of Victoria is dedicated to enhancing the lives of local farmers through comprehensive Agriculture Support Services. Whether you're a small-scale farmer, a cooperatively-run operation, or a new agricultural entrepreneur, we provide various programs and resources to ensure your agricultural activities are successful, sustainable, and profitable.

Our Agriculture Support Services focus on improving productivity, offering technical training, providing necessary inputs like seeds and fertilizers, and offering financial support to foster growth in the farming sector. We work closely with farmers to ensure they have the tools and knowledge to succeed in an evolving agricultural landscape.

  • Financial assistance and loans for agricultural development.
  • Technical training in modern farming techniques and technologies.
  • Distribution of quality seeds, fertilizers, and farm equipment.
  • Market linkage programs to connect local farmers with buyers.

With our support, we aim to ensure that every farmer in Victoria, Laguna can thrive by boosting productivity, adopting sustainable farming practices, and accessing new markets.
