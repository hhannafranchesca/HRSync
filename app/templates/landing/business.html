Supporting Local Businesses Through Efficient and Accessible Business Permit Services

The Municipality of Victoria, Laguna is committed to supporting local entrepreneurs and promoting sustainable economic growth through its streamlined and efficient Business Permit Services. Whether you're starting a new venture or renewing an existing business, we provide all the necessary assistance to make the process simple and hassle-free.

Our Business Permit and Licensing Office (BPLO) works closely with other local government departments to ensure that all business-related requirements—from documentation to inspections—are processed quickly and transparently. We aim to foster a business-friendly environment that encourages investment and economic activity within the community.

  • Quick and easy application for new and renewal of business permits.
  • Assistance with DTI Business Name Registration and Barangay Clearances.
  • Issuance of Sanitary Permits, Fire Safety Certificates, and Community Tax Certificates.
  • Business One-Stop Shop (BOSS) setup during peak seasons for faster processing.

Whether you're a small-scale vendor, a growing enterprise, or a micro business owner, our services are designed to help you operate legally and smoothly. We also provide guidance for BMBE (Barangay Micro Business Enterprise) certification to promote inclusive growth among grassroots businesses.

With the full support of the local government and a dedicated team at the BPLO, we ensure that doing business in Victoria is both rewarding and accessible. Let’s work together to build a thriving, business-friendly community.
